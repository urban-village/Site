Our Village Values: Kinship, Mutual learning, Inclusion, For us by us, Resilience, Solidarity.

KINSHIP- When we begin to identify with someone else, it no longer becomes a question if their humanity is worth fighting for, we never stop fighting for our own.

SHARING- We believe in the power of sharing resources, time, ideas, and support. We hope that what you receive will be reflective of what you give. Come give. Come receive.

MUTUAL LEARNING- Student or teacher. Mentor or mentee. We believe that we all have lessons to learn and something to teach. No one has the corner in this village. We learn alongside one another.

INCLUSION- No matter your background, your religion, your skin color, your gender identity, your ability, your sexual orientation, your education, your family history, or your personal story. You, are welcome in our village.

FOR US BY US- Though we partner with allys and outside support, all of The Urban Village programs are Karen dictated and Karen led.

RESILIENCE- We honor the resilience of the Karen people, who have known grit and perseverance in the face of great trials and injustice. So then, at The Urban Village we will be gritty, we will persevere, we will adapt, we will get flexible, and we will pass it on.

SOLIDARITY- We believe that justice is holistic and that “an injustice anywhere is a threat to justice anywhere” -MLK Jr. Therefore, as this organization hopes to work for justice, equality, and opportunity for the Karen people, it will always stand in solidarity with other marginalized people groups and other causes for justice. Always.
