Karen History Exhibit


About the Exhibit

In partnership with KOM (Karen Organization of Minnesota), the KOA (Karen Organization of America), and KCM (Karen Community of Minnesota) The Urban Village will play host to a Karen historical and cultural exhibit that will display the vibrant and ancient culture of the Karen people. With history, personal accounts, clothing, artifacts, paintings, and more, both Karen and non Karen people will be able to come and learn about the history and culture of the Karen people at this Urban Village exhibit.
