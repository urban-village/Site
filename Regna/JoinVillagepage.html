What we do for fun

Our goals at Urban Village are to help the KaRen community in any way possible. This might seem vague at first, but this makes a very rounded organization. If the community wants a place to look at their history or culture, we provide.

Programs

Being that we provide whatever the community desires, so if the community wants us to conduct english lessons, we provide. We can also establish lessons in doin the more logistical parts of American life, such as going to the bank, getting insurance, and getting a job.

Community Center

At the Community Center, we put on as many events as we can, everything from showcasing art to having games and activities.

Passage of stories

One of the important parts of the community that we strive for is the art of storytelling. Passing on a legacy and creating experiences that is fun to be part of is something we cherish.
