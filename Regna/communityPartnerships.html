Our Community Partnership Programs

Learn more about some of our community programs and other Karen community programs

Karen Networking Nights

The Urban Village provides resources and space to a young Karen professionals group who hold large group networking events as well as small groups and community forums.

Saw Kennedy’s Art School

Saw Kennedy is an artist from the Karen State in Burma who as an artist has inspired many and as an art teacher inspired that much more. We are fortunate to not only have some of Saw Kennedy’s art displayed on our walls, but his expertise to be shared within them. Stay tuned for dates and details on his upcoming classes in August.

The Local

A marketplace for Karen artist, entrepreneurs, and designers to share and sell their products.
