Our Village

Our Village is a community center located in a growing Karen in community in St. Paul Minnesota. Our Village focuses on providing the resources to the younger and old Karen community to ultimate create a better family dynamic. We provide educational tools while also providing the basic support the Karen community to thrive and properly function in America.

Mission Statement

Connection, Collaboration, Kinship

Core Values

Kinship, Mutual learning, Inclusion, For us by us, Resilience, Solidarity.
