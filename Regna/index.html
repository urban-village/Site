Our Village

The Urban Village is a Karen community space designed to cultivate connection, collaboration, and kinship both inside and outside its walls - both inside and outside the Karen Community. Our Family Academy Model works to enhance home-to-classroom engagement through the empowerment of parents and the support of students. At the Urban Village, we believe that the road to opportunity and achievement can be difficult to travel alone. We believe, it takes a village.
